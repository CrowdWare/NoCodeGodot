# CrowdFunding
In order to be able to finish the ForgePoser and Forge itself I want to raise some funds.
The idea is to finish the product, sell a commercial license and give away a non commercial open source license.
So the pledge will be such a commercial license.

This will be the start to make Forge public.
Other wonderful apps, ebooks and workshops based on Forge will follow.
And hopefully other dev will jump on the train and also build apps with Forge.
