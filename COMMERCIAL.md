# Commercial Licensing Guide

This guide explains when Forge can be used under GPL and when a commercial license is typically needed.

This document is informational and not legal advice.

## Licensing Model

- Forge source code is offered under `GPL-3.0-or-later`.
- Forge source code is also available under a commercial license from CrowdWare.
- SML and SMS are open languages. You can write and use SML/SMS files for your own projects.

## Typical Scenarios

1. Your users install Forge Runner themselves, and you only ship your SML/SMS project content.
   - Usually no commercial Forge license is needed from CrowdWare.
   - You still need to respect licenses of any third-party assets or dependencies you distribute.

2. You bundle or redistribute Forge code/runtime with your own product.
   - You can comply with GPL obligations and distribute under GPL terms.
   - If you want to keep your distributed product proprietary, obtain a commercial license from CrowdWare.

## Commercial License Contact

For commercial licensing inquiries, contact CrowdWare.
