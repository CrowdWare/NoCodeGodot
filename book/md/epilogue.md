## What This Book Is

This book is not a technical manual.
It is not a business strategy.
It is not a self-help guide.

It is a proof of concept.

Proof that a developer without a university degree can build something that matters.
Proof that thirty years is not wasted time – it is preparation time.
Proof that AI is a tool, not a replacement.
Proof that Ubuntu works in software as well as in life.
Proof that the future is not something that happens to you.
